Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Farm and Market

Agriculture supports nearly 45% of India's workforce, yet small and marginal farmers often struggle to receive fair prices for their crops. CropSync bridges this gap by creating a transparent, digital-first marketplace that connects farmers directly with buyers — eliminating middlemen, promoting fair trade, and empowering rural communities with technology.

The Challenge

Why Farmers Struggle Today

Despite being the backbone of our economy, farmers face systemic barriers that reduce their income and livelihood.

Middlemen Dependency

Farmers are forced to sell through intermediaries who take large cuts, drastically reducing the farmer's share of profit.

Unfair Pricing

Without price transparency, farmers often sell below MSP due to lack of market information and bargaining power.

Low Farmer Income

After paying intermediary fees, transport, and storage — the farmer's actual earnings are a fraction of the market price.

Lack of Transparency

Prices, buyer details, and market trends are often opaque, leaving farmers without the data they need to make informed decisions.

Our Solution

How CropSync Works

A simple, transparent, and farmer-first digital marketplace.

Direct Connection. Fair Pricing. Better Income.

CropSync removes the need for intermediaries by connecting farmers directly with buyers. Our platform integrates Government MSP data so both parties can make informed pricing decisions.
